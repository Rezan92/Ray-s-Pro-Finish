# Future Website Improvements

These are out-of-the-box, highly professional suggestions for the next phases of website development to maximize conversions and user trust.

## 1. Dedicated Individual Service Pages (SEO Goldmine)
Currently, we have one main `/services` page. To dominate local search results (SEO), we should create individual, highly detailed pages for:
- `/services/interior-painting`
- `/services/exterior-painting`
- `/services/commercial-painting`
This allows us to use targeted keywords on each page, answer specific questions about exterior paint durability, or commercial timelines, and rank significantly higher on Google.

## 2. Interactive "Before & After" Gallery Slider
Painting is a 100% visual industry. People buy transformations. We should create a custom **Interactive Image Slider Component** (where the user drags a line left and right to see the "Before" vs "After" of a room). We can put this on a dedicated `/projects` page or right on the Services page. This is the #1 way to instantly build trust.

## 3. Service Area & Local SEO Footer/Section
We need to clearly state *where* we work. Adding a dedicated section or map mentioning the exact cities/neighborhoods Ray's Pro Finish serves will instantly make local visitors feel confident in reaching out.

## 4. Floating / Sticky "Get a Quote" Button
As visitors scroll through these beautiful, long pages (especially on mobile), they shouldn't have to scroll all the way back to the top or bottom to contact us. Implementing a persistent, sticky "Get a Free Quote" button that follows them as they scroll drastically increases conversion rates.
